About Unmasked

Unmasked is a cutting-edge AI solution designed to detect face-swap deepfake videos with precision and speed. Our mission is to help people and organizations maintain trust in media by identifying digital manipulations in videos.

Our Mission

In an era of increasing misinformation, we are committed to providing a trustworthy tool for detecting deepfake videos. Our platform leverages the latest advancements in AI and machine learning to ensure accurate detection.

Unmasked offers seamless support for various video formats, allowing users to upload, analyze, and receive detailed reports on video authenticity in real-time.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where digital content can be trusted and relied upon. We aspire to lead the way in the fight against digital misinformation, ensuring that individuals and organizations can navigate the digital landscape with confidence. By continuously improving our detection methods and expanding our reach, we aim to make Unmasked a global standard in deepfake detection.

We believe in empowering users with the tools and knowledge to identify manipulated content, fostering a future where authenticity is valued and protected. Our goal is to not only detect deepfakes but to educate the public on the potential dangers of misinformation in the digital age.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of AI experts, cybersecurity professionals, and developers working tirelessly to combat the spread of deepfake technology. Through continuous innovation, we aim to build tools that enhance media transparency and trust.
